On February 9 2018, the barangay councilors opens a free vaccination for pets. Not only for dogs but also for cats. This is to support not only animal welfare but also the safety and health of the community. But the project is limited due to the number of vaccines that was provided for the barangay. A 150 injectibles vaccines was provided by the government of Baguio City. Some people are now well-prepared and excited about this project because of love for their pets.
