Teachers Camp

 Teacher's Camp is one of the most preserved places here in Baguio. Not only preserved, it is also well-mantained when it comes to cleanliness and the beauty of environment. It is one of the tourist's spots here in Baguio that's why many people especially students from other provinces and cities chooses to stay in Teacher's Camp which serves as their field trip.

Mission

To spread awareness and inform people or persons living in this barangay about the latest news/happening, rules and events on how to protect and preserve the environment in the barangay teachers camp.

Vission

We envision a clean, beautiful, healthy and well protected environment of the barangay teachers camp, where a person, family and the future generation can live and enjoy the beautiful creation of our dear Lord.

Motto

Protect and Preserve! for the future generation.
